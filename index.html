Reflect

Reflect is a therapeutic journaling app that encourages users to return their old entries and reflect upon what they had previously written. A user can gain a new perspective on their entry by viewing it as a word cloud. Technologies: Rails, Materialize Ruby Gems: Bcrypt, WordsCounted, jqCloud 1, Kaminari Languages: Ruby, jquery, Javascript HTML5, SASS
