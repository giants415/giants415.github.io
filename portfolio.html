Disneyland ProTips

Concept
A vacation to Disneyland can turn into a very expensive and stressful trip without the proper planning. Disneyland ProTips was built to solve this issue by creating a meeting point for novice Disneyland visitors to research and ask for advice from the people that visit the most.

Stack: MongoDB, Express.JS, Node.JS, Bootstrap 3

Reflect

Concept
Reflect was developed as a journal with tools to emphasize and encourage users to reflect upon what they previously wrote. When creating a new entry, users rate their mood before and after writing. Each entry displays their respective scores after submission and, additionally, is used to calculate average mood ratings that are displayed on the user dashboard. Finally, users can "cloudify" a post to view their writing as a word cloud.

Stack: Rails, Materialize

Languages: HTML5, Ruby, SASS
